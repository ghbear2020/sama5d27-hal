Information Center for ARM | EXAMPLES

Description: The iso7816 software provide in this examples is use to transform APDU commands to TPDU commands for the smart card. The iso7816 provide here is for the protocol T=0 only. The send and the receive of a character is made under polling. In the file ISO7816_Init is defined all pins of the card. User must have to change this pins according to his environment. The driver is compliant with CASE 1, 2, 3 of the ISO7816-4 specification.
